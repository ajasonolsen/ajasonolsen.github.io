Din Djarin

aka ‘The Mandalorian’ or ‘Mando’

BHG (4 ABY - present)
Navarro

I am always looking for lucrative work, yet lately I’ve been distracted by managing an “asset.” The experience has forced me to lighten up a bit. Lately I have been open to interacting more with others, have stopped hating on droids so much, and have wondered what a helmet-less life may bring.

That being said, I still enjoy long flights into the universe and carbon freezing criminals when they step out of line.

My friends call me Pedro.

Work Experiences

I have experiences working with and against all kinds of characters throughout the galaxy. Here are a few examples of the personalities I have encountered:

Ice Spiders

Found skittering in frozen caverns are knobby white ice spiders that can swarm their prey and feast with their razor-sharp proboscices. These creatures can spin webs of ice strands to trap their quarries.

Imperial Juggernaut Driver

Imperial combat drivers operate the Empire's Ground Assault Vehicles (GAVs) -- everything from troop transports to heavily armored titans like the Juggernaut turbo tank. Combat drivers are lightly armored, relying on the thick skin of their vehicles to protect them in battle.

Gor Koresh

A low-life gangster on a lawless world, Gor Koresh is a gambler who profits from cruel underground gladiator matches.

Meet the team

Even though I have mad skills, my friends offer up some skillful help from time to time.


Peli Motto

The scrappy proprietor of Hangar 3-5 in the Mos Eisley Spaceport, Peli Motto makes a living refueling and repairing battered ships. With the help of a team of hyperactive pit droids, she can make quick work of the worst carbon scoring, but she's willing to tackle the job herself for an additional fee.

Greef Karga

Once an expeditor for the Bounty Hunters Guild, Greef Karga ran the trade on Nevarro, a middle-man, a connector between clients and bounty hunters. Now Magistrate of Nevarro, Greef is cleaning up the rough-and-tumble world with the help of Marshal Cara Dune.

Fennec Shand

An assassin and elite mercenary, Fennec Shand has worked for all the top syndicates. Clever, capable, and cunning, she is not to be underestimated and is full of surprises.
