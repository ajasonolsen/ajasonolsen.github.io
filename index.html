Spanish Tofu Scramble

April 7, 2025

Servings: 3-4

Ingredients

2 tablespoons of neutral oil, such as grape seed or avocado 1/2 red, white, or yellow onion, small diced 1/2 green or red bell pepper, small diced

Instructions

Spoon your Spanish tofu scramble on a plate, add suggested toppings. Serve with warm tortillas. Chef’s TIP: Add the tomatoes after the tofu so they stay fresh.
